Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

1. Information We Collect

When you place an order, we collect only the minimum information necessary to fulfill it:

  • Name — to identify your order
  • Email address — to send order confirmations and updates
  • Phone number (optional) — only if you provide it for delivery coordination

We do not collect payment card details. All payments are processed through our third-party crypto payment provider.

2. How We Use Your Data

Your personal information is used exclusively for:

  • Processing and fulfilling your order
  • Sending order status notifications
  • Responding to customer support inquiries

We will never use your data for marketing purposes unless you explicitly opt in.

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise share your personal information with third parties, except:

  • Shipping carriers — your name and address to deliver your package
  • Payment processors — the transaction amount (no personal details)
  • Legal requirements — if required by law or court order

4. Cookies & Session Data

We use a minimal session cookie to maintain your shopping cart while browsing. This cookie:

  • Contains no personal information
  • Is deleted when you close your browser
  • Is essential for the store to function (cannot be disabled)

We do not use tracking cookies, analytics, or advertising pixels.

5. Data Retention

Order data is retained only for the duration necessary to complete your order and handle any returns or disputes. Session data (shopping cart) is temporary and automatically expires.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request access to your personal data
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Withdraw consent at any time

7. Security

We implement industry-standard security measures including CSRF protection, secure session handling, input validation, and content security policies to protect your information during transmission and storage.

8. Contact

For any privacy-related questions or data requests, please contact us through the store's support channels.
